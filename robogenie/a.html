About Course

SYLLABUS

Session Structure of Course

SESSION-1
INTRODUCTION to SPEED THINKING AND SPEED SOLVING

  • Fundamentals of Speed Mathematics - Left to Right System - Trachtenberg’s methods
  • Video Demonstrations of Calculations by Neelakantha Bhanu for motivating students
  • Speed and Accuracy assessment – Pre course
  • Activity I

Session 2 & 3 - ARITHMETIC BUILDING MODULE - SPEED MATHEMATICS

  • High speed additions through visual interface and experiential learning modules.
  • Skill Assessment through worksheets
  • Speed Assessment (+)
  • High Speed Multiplication – understanding the concept of multiplication, quicker methods to multiply.
  • Speed Assessment (x)
  • Activity II

Session 4 & 5 - COGNITIVE ABILITY MODULE I

  • Cog Ability Tracker 1.0, records the students cognitive parameters like - memory retention, working memory, through Arithmetic Game I
  • Trainer Demonstration
  • Second-Interval Training

Session 6 & 7 - SHARPENING MEMORY MODULE

  • Memory Training - Memory Train
  • Peg Method of Memory
  • Mind Palace - SHERLOCK!
  • Using Mind Palace to Partition Thought

Session 8 - THEORETICAL LEARNING

  • Visualising Fractions & Decimals
  • Division – building precision
  • A introduction to Algebra and Variables
  • Direction Sense
  • Interpreting Percentages and Ratios
  • Cog Ability Tracker 1.0 - Arithmetic Game II

Session 9 & 10 - SENSORY PRECISION AND PARALLEL THINKING

  • Audio based question generating software, focussing on listening skill development
  • Real-World Problem Solving session
  • Cog Ability Tracker 1.0 - Arithmetic Game III
  • Speed assessment post course
  • Partitioning Thought and Parallel Thinking Exercises

About the Course Instructor

Neelakantha Bhanu Prakash neelakanthabhanu.com/, the Fastest Human Calculator will be taking up this course, personally. Bhanu holds 4 World Records and 50 Limca Book records and is a TEDx speaker and educator. He has led projects in developing arithmetic skills in students across the globe.

Bhanu’s proficiency in Speed Math and Brain Training guarantees quality delivery. Under him, Exploring Infinities has reached out to 10000+ students in India, Bangladesh, Nepal and Indonesia. He has addressed audiences in the GEB (Jakarta), International School Awards, YSI Bangladesh, Future City Summit (China) and was invited as a speaker to 50+ conferences including pioneer institutions like ISB, T-Hub and IIT to speak about the importance of arithmetic learning in overall development of a child.
