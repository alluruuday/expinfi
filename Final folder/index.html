About us

At Exploring Infinities, we believe that the way math is learned, practiced has a direct impact on a child’s cognitive improvement.

Cognitive Ability Parameters Know-How

Being an active believer in Body is just a puppet of a healthy brain. You only need to train the brain, we wanted to propose something path-breaking yet fun for people in your evolving school. We believe that, the improving a child in the following cognitive parameters helps in holistic development. We use arithmetic learning and games as the foundation on which we help students improve in these cognitive parameters and also measure the improvement.

Computation

The ability to dynamically process inputs is a computation. Spped Calculations, speed thinking are outcomes of an improved computation skill.

Working Memory

Working memory is a cognitive system with a limited capacity that can hold information temporarily. It is important for reasoning and the guidance of decision-making and behavior.

Visualization

The arrangement of thoughts and processes in your head is your visualization skill. Spiked creativity, critical thinking are outcomes of an improved visualization.

Reflex

The reflex is then an automatic response to a stimulus that does not receive or need conscious thought.

Sensory Precision

The precision of our visual and auditory senses is sensory precision. Sensory Precision as a factor, captures the attention parameter of an individual.

Time Perception

Refers to the subjective experience of time, which is measured by one's own perception of the duration. Improved perception of time, develops concentration and attention.

Long Term Memory

Long-term memory refers to the storage of information over an extended period. The information stored here is like the information stored on a hard drive of a computer.

Skills of Interest

A few skills which are developed in students who take up cognitive ability training :

Speed Thinking

The skill of thinking and processing quickly. This can be inculcated with by improving computation and sensory precision parameters.

Speed Mathematics

The ability to perform quick calculations. This is achieved by improving working memory, visualisation and computation parameters in students.

Parallel Thinking

Thinking about two things at the same time & managing distractions. Improved computation, visualisation and time perception can be used to Partition Thought and Parallel Think.

Quantification Logic

Ability to weigh down two alternatives, by vivid imagination, quick computations and arriving at a logical decision.

Memory Retention

Retaining memory for longer periods of time is an important skill. Delving deep into the mind palace, association methods, we will make memory a piece of cake.
