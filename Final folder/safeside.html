About us

At Exploring Infinities, we believe that the way math is learned, practiced has a direct impact on a child’s cognitive improvement.

Exploring Infinities, founded by Neelakantha Bhanu Prakash - The World’s Fastest Human Calculator works with students and work professionals across different educational and professional institutions in enabling cognitive ability development and numeracy development.
