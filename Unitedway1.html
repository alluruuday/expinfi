United Way - Aqriti Scholars - Mathematics and Logic Building Intervention

NIMSME, Hyderabad - 2020

Aqriti Scholarship is awarded to meritorious students who are from underprivileged backgrounds in the government schools of Telangana State. This fellowship is administered by Qualcomm, in partnership with UnitedWay Hyderabad.

Exploring Infinities, partnered with United Way assessed the numerical ability, speed and logic, conducted 4 sessions in training them in the same.

It began with Bhanu introducing himself, sharing his story to inspire the students to take up mathematics and strive to be better at it. In this session he understood from the students about the problems they face in general arithmetic and specific kinds of problems they would like to be quick at. It started with an informal ice-breaker session with the students discussing their aspirations, how they want to get there and their general interest towards mathematics and numbers We later had the assessment where the students were asked to solve 6 questions.

Taking the results of the first session into consideration, the second session was focused towards making the students more accurate in the tasks of multiplication because multiplication forms the basis of the majority of higher mathematics and the students HAVE to do better.Out of the 396 questions solved in both Pre-Evaluation and Post-Evaluation Assessments by the entire class, the number of correct answers increased from 202 to 251.

A few students like Mahjabeen Begum, R Mounica, Nazia Begum had almost 83% increase in their accuracy.

The average accuracy of a student for completing the task before the training = 51.8%

The average accuracy of a student for completing the task after the training = 64.3%

Which is almost 1.3x before and 12.5% more accurate than what the students were before the training was done. This showcases the impact of the training session.
